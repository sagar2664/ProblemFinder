Each cell has a belt, and all belts are initially facing to the right.Initially, a slime ball is on the belt of $$$(0, 0)$$$, and other belts are empty. Every second, the state of the conveyor changes as follows:   All slime balls on the conveyor move one cell in the direction of the belt at the same time.
