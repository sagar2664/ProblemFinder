Find the biggest $$$x$$$, such that $$$f(a, x)$$$ is a palindrome.Here, $$$a \bmod x$$$ is the remainder of the integer division of $$$a$$$ by $$$x$$$.An array is a palindrome if it reads the same backward as forward.
