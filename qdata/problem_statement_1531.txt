For example, $$$[2,3,1,5,4]$$$ is a permutation, but $$$[1,2,2]$$$ is not a permutation ($$$2$$$ appears twice in the array) and $$$[1,3,4]$$$ is also not a permutation ($$$n=3$$$ but there is $$$4$$$ in the array).You are given a permutation of $$$1,2,\dots,n$$$, $$$[a_1,a_2,\dots,a_n]$$$.
