To achieve this, he will flip some of the light switches at the exit of the warehouse, but since Nikola is lazy, he wants to flip the _minimum_ number of switches required to turn all the lights off. During a period of $$$D$$$ days, Nikola noted which light bulbs were off and which were on at the end of each day.
